## npm

Finnish government cybersecurity standards MCP -- Julkri, Katakri, NCSC-FI guidelines, Tietosuojavaltuutetun ohjeet, VAHTI, Kanta health services security. Part of the Ansvar MCP Network (ansvar.ai/mcp).

## Smithery

Access Finnish government cybersecurity standards including Julkri (DVV), Katakri (Puolustusministerio), NCSC-FI guidelines (Traficom), data protection guidance, VAHTI, and Kanta health services requirements. Bilingual (Finnish + English) with FTS search, ISO 27002 cross-references, and sector-based filtering.

## Glama

Finnish government cybersecurity standards MCP covering 6 frameworks and 253 controls. Search by keyword, sector, or ISO mapping. Bilingual Finnish/English support.
